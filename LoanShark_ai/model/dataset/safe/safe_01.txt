LOAN AGREEMENT (SAMPLE) — SAFE

Loan Amount: $2,000
Annual Percentage Rate (APR): 14%

Fees:
Origination Fee: 1% of loan amount
Late Fee: $15 if payment is more than 10 days late

Repayment:
Term: 12 months
Payment Schedule: Monthly payments due on the 5th of each month
Prepayment: No prepayment penalty. Borrower may pay early without additional charges.

Default:
If Borrower misses a payment, Lender may assess the late fee stated above.

Dispute Resolution:
Disputes may be resolved in a court of competent jurisdiction. Borrower retains all legal rights.

Authorization:
Lender may not debit Borrower’s account without explicit consent for each payment.
